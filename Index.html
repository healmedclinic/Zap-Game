What is TMS Therapy?

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) is a non-invasive, FDA-approved treatment that uses magnetic fields to stimulate specific areas of the brain associated with depression, anxiety, and addiction.

Key Benefits:

  • Non-invasive and medication-free
  • No systemic side effects
  • Outpatient treatment
  • Proven effectiveness

Treatment Process

What to Expect

Session Duration

Each session lasts 20-40 minutes. You'll remain awake and alert throughout the treatment.

Treatment Schedule

Typically 5 sessions per week for 4-6 weeks, with some protocols extending longer.

Side Effects

Mild headache or scalp discomfort that typically resolves quickly after treatment.
